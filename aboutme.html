WHO IS ZANDI?

Driven by a lifelong dream of emulating TV actors, Zandi sought to entertain audiences. At 11, he approached his parents, who supported his acting aspirations. Navigating the industry independently, he joined an acting site which proved costly with limited paid opportunities. Transitioning to middle school, Zandi participated in musicals and plays, later pursuing formal training at LaGuardia Arts, where frequent shows and scene shares provide ample performance opportunities.
