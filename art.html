PERFORMANCE

Driven by a love for music and singing, Zandi's journey into acting began unexpectedly. Initially believing that everyone was naturally gifted at singing, he realized his own talent in fifth grade when a new music teacher allowed students to audition for solos in seasonal concerts. This experience motivated him to pursue entertainment, leading him to join the choir despite previous reservations. The performance opportunities and solos he gained fueled his passion for music and served as a gateway to his acting career, particularly in musical theater.
